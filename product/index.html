Big Ideas

by Bryce Thompson

We truly are still at the beginning of an amazing digital revolution with so much opportunity to make the lives of everyday people better. Many startups and entrepreneurs have seen this need. Many have succeeded, and many have failed trying to be the first to every new and emerging market in need of their processes being ported to digital. Innovation is happening in the client-side viewport and now, how you execute in Prototyping, UX/UI Design, and UI Development determine much of your project's success with your audience.

  • UX/UI Design

However, to get there, traditionally often takes a great amount of risk or upfront investment in order to get the right solution or your most viable product. Design for a long time and for that matter, interactive design, has even been slow to assimilate many of the efficiencies found in development processes, and that’s still somewhat common to encounter.

03

UX to Investment

Product Design isn’t just for Commercial, but also for Business to Business markets because we know that it requires a human-centered, yet commercial appeal to win the hearts of minds of your stakeholders before development involvement is required. Being meticulous at every detail in design production makes it possible to get as close to primetime full-fledged deployment with relatively low investment when compared to waterfall processes and more specialized design teams.

04

Design You Can Build With

Delivering on UI Kits which have explicit dimension attributes and Quartz files that have real applicable values make developing your production application easy, and allows developers to focus on the things that make an experience truly amazing which is the extensibility, optimization, and scalability of the stack.

05

Beautiful Prototyping

Using tools like Quartz Composer/Origami, Invision, and being skilled in production UI development means single Product Designers can travel down the path to MVP faster than many larger teams. Hiring a team of a designers and production front-end people is expensive, and product designers offer the best of many distinct worlds.

06

Production Static Dev

Because of many product designer experiences, they are able to interface the best with talented developers by virtue of being versed in javascript frameworks such as AngularJS, ReactJS, and jQuery which is where much of the so-called magic happens. In reality, it’s where a lot of the great data calculations in the background meet the needs of the user. For some, as is the case with this studio, we’re able to create production ready static files.

07

Data-Driven Means Better Experience

When we know about a person, their behavior, what drives them to use something, and the when, where, when, how and why’s of a usecase then you can iterate the product to the next level. Product Designers love data, it mitigates the risk on a design decision and a lot of times leads to a more unique, and efficient solution which is the goal of every project. Products that are uniquely efficient, but somehow empathise greatly with their operators. Truly Product Design Nirvana.

Start a Modern Day

Digital Renaissance

The digital landscape has perhaps spurred on a 2nd Enlightenment in the sense that the new Renaissance Man or Woman exists in the digital world solving the problems of many disciplines. Questioning principles, adapting to an ever-changing scenery of innovation and progress. This is what largely makes up Product Design, and why it’s so crucial to apply a multi disciplinarian lense to problems that are always changing in nature.

All of these tools and processes add up to a solution that not only meet the demands of your startup, but will also put companies on stable footing to evolve a product many iterations down the line. When it’s so important to be first to market with an idea, it’s so crucial to find the right product designer to craft a digital product.
